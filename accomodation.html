Our rooms

This amazing tropical resort has an assortment of rooms which cater for the varying needs of the guests. Our presidential suite room consists of A big double bed, a 55 inch mounted flatscreen, reading desk, balcony with seats, jacuzzi and hot shower and a safe for storing valuables. This suite is priced at Ksh 50000 per night inclusive of bed and breakfast
