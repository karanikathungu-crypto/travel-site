Why Mkingoni?

Mkingoni Resort is a five star tropical resort located in the equatorial region of Nyunaki offering accomodation services at affordable rates that are pocket friendly to the adventurer planning to stay for a short time, the business man on a trip to attend meetings, the couple on a honeymoon retreat and the family on a bonding vacation. Mkingoni takes care of all those needs taking a holistic approach in provision of services.
